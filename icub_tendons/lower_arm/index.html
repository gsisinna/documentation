    Definition of "Lower Arm"¶

    We define the "Lower Arm" as the assembly of the Forearm and the Hand.

    Definition

    LOWER ARM = FOREARM + HAND

    The Lower Arm is composed of 12 motors (Forearm: 10 + Hand: 2)

    Let's describe the functionality of those motors:

    Forearm: (10)

    Pronosupination (1)

    Wrist: (2) = pan + tilt

    Thumb: (2) = proximal + distal

    Index: (2) = proximal + distal

    Middle: (2) = proximal + distal

    Pinky-ring fingers: (1) = distal

    Hand: (2)

    Fingers abduction (1)

    Thumb oppose (1)
